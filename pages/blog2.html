Thursday Blog

On Thursday we had a talk with Rachel, which was initially meant to be abot Maximising Internships, but ended up being aboout her path in the industry, and how most tech companies will tend to look for the person who they believe will work the best with their team-who can ask for help, and give it- as opposed to the most technically capable one. After that we had a talk with Paulo, a Redhat employee in Italy, who managed to include his passion (Formula 1 Racing) with his job at Redhat, by using telemetry data from Formula Xbox games. I liked this talk as it opened my eyes to how you can apply your passionto your job. At the end of the day we had a final workshop with Christian, where we worked on a basic game, fixing bugs and customizing it to our liking.
